This recommendation value is only for 2-row malting barley.

Nitrogen recommendation in this app is based on an economic production function that takes into account the yield response of barley to added N, less the cost of the N.

The final Nitrogen recommendation is the average optimal rate. Growers may choose to apply up to 15 lb N/acre more or less than the calculated N rates due to protein traits of a variety, special soil conditions such as susceptibility to spring denitrification, application techniques that may not be most efficient or historical experiences from the field or part of a field that may influence N uptake and efficiency.
